Welcome to ShadowSyndicate!

Build your criminal empire, automate operations, and become the most powerful syndicate boss. This page contains helpful information and answers to common questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play the game?

Tap on operations to generate income, hire gangsters to boost your production, and purchase upgrades to increase your earnings. The game continues to earn money even when you're offline!

Does the game work offline?

Yes! ShadowSyndicate works completely offline. Your syndicate continues earning even when the app is closed. You'll collect all your offline earnings when you return to the game.

How does the Prestige system work?

When you reach certain milestones, you can reset your progress in exchange for prestige points. These points give you permanent bonuses that make each subsequent run faster and more powerful.

What are gangsters and how do I use them?

Gangsters are specialists (hackers, burglars, drivers, etc.) that boost your operations. Each gangster multiplies your earnings from specific operations. Hire them strategically to maximize your income!

How do I maximize my earnings?

Focus on upgrading your most profitable operations first. Balance between hiring gangsters and upgrading operations. Don't forget to collect your offline earnings regularly!

I lost my progress! What happened?

Your progress is saved locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, your progress will be lost. Make sure to keep the app installed to maintain your save data.

Is there a way to backup my progress?

Currently, all saves are local to your device. Make sure not to delete the app or clear its data to keep your progress safe.

Are there in-app purchases?

ShadowSyndicate is designed to be enjoyed without requiring purchases. All features are accessible through gameplay.

The game is running slow. What should I do?

Try closing other apps running in the background. If problems persist, try restarting your device. Make sure you're running the latest version of the game.
