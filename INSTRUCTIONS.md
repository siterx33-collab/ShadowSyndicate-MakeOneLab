## üöÄ Step-by-Step Upload Instructions

### Step 1: Create GitHub Account (if you don't have one)

---

### Step 2: Create a New Repository
1. Once logged in, click the **"+"** icon (top right) ‚Üí **"New repository"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Repository name:** `shadowsyndicate-support` (or any name you like)
   - **Description:** "Support and privacy pages for ShadowSyndicate"
   - **Public** (must be public for GitHub Pages to work)
   - ‚úÖ Check "Add a README file"
3. Click **"Create repository"**

---

### Step 3: Upload Your HTML Files
1. In your new repository, click **"Add file"** ‚Üí **"Upload files"**
2. Drag and drop BOTH files:
   - `privacy.html`
   - `support.html`
3. Scroll down and click **"Commit changes"**

---

### Step 4: Enable GitHub Pages
1. In your repository, click **"Settings"** (top menu)
2. Scroll down the left sidebar and click **"Pages"**
3. Under "Source":
   - Select **"Deploy from a branch"**
   - Branch: Select **"main"**
   - Folder: **"/ (root)"**
4. Click **"Save"**
5. Wait 1-2 minutes for deployment

---

### Step 5: Get Your URLs
After GitHub Pages is enabled, you'll see a message like:
"Your site is live at https://yourusername.github.io/shadowsyndicate-support/"

Your final URLs will be:
- **Support URL:** `https://yourusername.github.io/shadowsyndicate-support/support.html`
- **Privacy URL:** `https://yourusername.github.io/shadowsyndicate-support/privacy.html`

(Replace "yourusername" with your actual GitHub username)
